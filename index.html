Lemonada

When Andreea met Lemonada for the first time, she was starving and needed help. As a feral cat, Lemonada was not willing to trust humans and kept her distance. It took Andreea arduous work and patience to finally earn her trust. The process was long and difficult, but it was so rewarding at the end that it made everything worthwhile.

Pic & Poc

A motorist noticed two little cats in the middle of the highway. Dodging traffic, he rescued them. The people tried to bottle feed them, but Pic and Poc bit off part of the nipple and swallowed it! A trip to the emergency vet revealed rocks and pebbles in their stomach as well. Can you imagine a kitten so starved that it would eat rocks?
